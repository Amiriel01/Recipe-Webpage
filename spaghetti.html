Spaghetti

Pictured: Spaghetti topped with a meat sauce, herbs, and parmesan cheese.

If you're looking for a robust homecooked meal this dish is for you! This flavorful spaghetti sauce pairs well with angel hair or spaghetti pasta. Served with a side of garlic bread and a salad to create the perfect meal.

You can make this recipe in under an hour using store bought ingredients. This sauce also freezes well if you'd like to make a double recipe and save half for later!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. Ground Beef
  • 1 Jar Ragu Traditional Sauce
  • 1 tbsp. Italian Seasoning
  • 2 c. Shredded Mozzerella Cheese
  • 1 tsp. Garlic Powder
  • 1 tsp. Onion Powder
  • 16 oz. Angel Hair or Spaghetti Noodles
  • Grated Paremesan Cheese, Optional

Steps

  1. Place a large pot on the stove with water to boil for the noodles.
  2. Brown ground beef in a large skillet.
  3. Add garlic powder, onion powder, and italian seasoning to the ground been while browning it.
  4. Drain the oil from the ground beef once it is finished cooking then return it to the skillet.
  5. Add the pasta to the pot when the water starts boiling. Boil the noodles for 8 minutes, stirring occasionally. Drain the water once the noodles are done.
  6. Add the Ragu Traditional sauce to the ground beef and stir well.
  7. Simmer the sauce for 15 minutes.
  8. Add the Mozzerella cheese to the sauce and ground beef mixture and stir till melted.
  9. Top the cooked noodles with the completed spaghetti sauce and top with parmesan cheese if desired.
